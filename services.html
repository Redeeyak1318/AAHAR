Our Services

What We Offer

At AAHAR, we bring the soul of Assam beyond our dining room. From grand wedding feasts to intimate cultural evenings, our services are designed to immerse you in the warmth, flavours, and traditions of Assamese hospitality.

Catering

Traditional Wedding & Event Catering

Let AAHAR bring the authentic grandeur of Assamese cuisine to your most cherished celebrations. Our wedding and event catering service is a carefully curated culinary journey — from the auspicious Khar that opens the feast to the sweet Pitha that closes it.

Every dish is prepared with heritage recipes, organic ingredients, and the traditional cooking methods that have been the hallmark of Assamese hospitality for generations.

  • Full-service Assamese wedding menus (200–2000+ guests)
  • Traditional bell-metal & banana leaf serving setup
  • Live cooking stations with master chefs
  • Custom menus for Bihu, Durga Puja & cultural events
  • Gamosa-draped décor & marigold styling

Events

Bihu Special Feast & Cultural Nights

Experience the vibrant spirit of Assam through our seasonal events. Our Bihu Special Feasts are a celebration of harvest, joy, and community — featuring authentic festive menus paired with live Bihu music, traditional dance performances, and the warmth of Assamese togetherness.

Our Cultural Nights transport you to the heart of Assam with curated evenings of storytelling, folk music, and a multi-course heritage dinner that celebrates the diverse culinary traditions of the Brahmaputra valley.

  • Rongali, Kongali & Bhogali Bihu themed feasts
  • Live dhol & pepa performances
  • Traditional Bihu dance by local artists
  • Cultural storytelling & Assamese folk music nights
  • Festive menu with seasonal delicacies

Private Dining

Exclusive Ethnic Dining Experience

Step into a world of intimate luxury with our private ethnic dining experience. Set within bamboo-adorned private rooms illuminated by traditional oil lamps, every detail is crafted to transport you to the serene villages and lush landscapes of Assam.

Ideal for milestone celebrations, corporate gatherings, or a deeply personal culinary journey — our private dining offers a bespoke multi-course menu tailored to your palate, served on traditional bell-metal ware with the grace of Assamese hospitality.

  • Intimate bamboo & cane-themed private rooms
  • Bespoke 7-course heritage tasting menus
  • Personal chef & dedicated service team
  • Premium Assam tea & local beverage pairings
  • Perfect for 2–20 guests
