Our Story

Since 2011

A Legacy of Assamese Flavours

AAHAR was born from a deep love for the culinary traditions of Assam — a land of lush tea gardens, mighty rivers, and a cuisine that is as diverse as its culture.

Founded by Chef Bhaskar Hazarika, our restaurant brings together family recipes passed through generations, using traditional techniques like slow-cooking in bell metal vessels and flavouring with indigenous herbs from the Brahmaputra valley.

Every dish at AAHAR is a tribute to Assamese heritage — from the tangy Masor Tenga to the festive Til Pitha, each plate tells a story of our roots.

Cultural Heritage

The Soul of Assamese Cuisine

Assamese cuisine is a celebration of simplicity and flavour. Rooted in the bountiful produce of the Brahmaputra plains, it is characterized by minimal use of spices, allowing the natural taste of fresh ingredients to shine.

From the use of Khar (a unique alkaline ingredient) to cooking with banana leaves and bamboo hollows, the techniques are as fascinating as the flavours they produce.

At AAHAR, we preserve these traditions while presenting them with a touch of modern elegance — honouring the past while inviting the future to the table.
